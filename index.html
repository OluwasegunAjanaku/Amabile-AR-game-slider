Phase 1: Scan & Unlock

Marker-Based Gating

The AR lens gameplay loop triggers immediately upon camera detection of either:

  • Physical Amabile di Rosa Wine Bottle Image target recognition mapped to the elegant bottle neck or front label contours.
  • Distinctive Brand Logo Print Asset High-contrast logo marker printed on coasters, retail stands, or digital screens.

Drives active product interaction directly at point of sale, retail shelves, and dinner tables.

Phase 5: The Peer Verdict

Social Evaluation System

To eliminate passive observation, all non-active session members form an immediate interactive jury panel:

  • Legit (Green Option) Validates honesty, successful dare execution, or courageous disclosure.
  • Cap !!! (Red Option) Punishes avoidance, fake confessions, or clear task dodging.

Forces constant active participation, turning the AR lens from a solo trick into a shared group theater.

Snap Game ADR-_User-Profile.jpg Jury feedback sliders showing real-time truth indices and total Legit/Cap ratios for each player.

Phase 6: Stakes & Rewards

Real-World Commerce Integration

The Lens engine calculates absolute totals at session close, establishing distinct real-world stakes that link back to the brand:

  • The Winner The player who holds the highest collective "Legit" metric and successfully completed prompts.
  • The Loser (Sponsor Gateway) Officially branded to "Owe the Winner a Bottle of Amabile Sweet Wine."

An integrated call-to-action button links straight to retail partners or fast delivery apps to fulfill the bottle pledge!
